## 📖 Course Description

Industry 4.0 concerns the transformation of industrial processes through the
integration of modern technologies such as **sensors, communication, and
computational processing**.

Key driving technologies include:

- 🔗 **Cyber Physical Systems (CPS)**
- 🌐 **Internet of Things (IoT)**
- ☁️ **Cloud Computing**
- 🤖 **Machine Learning**
- 📊 **Data Analytics**

**Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT)** is an application of IoT in industries
that modifies various existing industrial systems —
linking automation with enterprise planning and product lifecycle management.

## 🎯 Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, you will be able to:

- Understand the **fundamentals of Industry 4.0** and its key driving technologies
- Explain the **architecture and layers of IIoT** systems
- Describe **IIoT sensing, processing, communication and networking**
- Apply **Big Data Analytics and Machine Learning** concepts to industrial data
- Understand **SDN, Cloud and Fog Computing** in industrial contexts
- Identify **cybersecurity challenges** in Industry 4.0 environments
- Explore **real-world IIoT applications** across healthcare, power, manufacturing and more
