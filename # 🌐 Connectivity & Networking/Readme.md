# 🌐 Domain 01 — Sensing, Connectivity & Networking

Foundational concepts behind how industrial systems sense,
communicate and exchange information.

This domain explores the technologies that enable physical assets,
machines and industrial processes to become observable, connected
and digitally accessible.

---

## Why This Matters

Connectivity is the foundation of Industry 4.0.

Without reliable sensing, communication and networking,
higher-level capabilities such as analytics, AI, traceability
and smart manufacturing cannot exist.
