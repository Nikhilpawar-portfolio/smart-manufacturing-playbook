Terms are documented in practitioner language — emphasizing how they appear in projects, assessments, implementations and industrial conversations rather than textbook definitions.

## How This Glossary Works

Each term typically includes:

- **Definition** — concise explanation
- **Why It Matters** — operational significance
- **Where It Appears** — industries, applications or projects
- **Related Concepts** — connected topics worth exploring
- **First Mentioned In** — topic or entry where it surfaced
